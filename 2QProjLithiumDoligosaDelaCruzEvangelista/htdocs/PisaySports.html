Citymeet tryouts

Last Wednesday, November 29, 2023, was the start of the citymeet tryouts. Sports like basketball, volleyball, football, badminton, taekwondo, tennis and many other sports held their tryouts.

Wonder what the most played sports of PSHS-WVC scholars are?!

Most scholars can be seen playing in the oval, quadrangle or the gym! The most popular sports are: basketball, football, volleyball, frisbee and badminton.
