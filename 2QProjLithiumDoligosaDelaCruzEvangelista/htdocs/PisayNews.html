What caused this budget cut?

The Philippine Science High School System suffered a budget cut in the proposed 2024 funding under the National Expenditure Program. The budget cut was due to non-recurring and terminating locally-funded projects.

How much was deducted from the budget?

300M pesos was deducted from the PSHSS. In PSHS-WVC, around 70M pesos was deducted.

Are the scholars affected by this?

According to the CID chief of PSHS-WVC, Sir Lando Libutaque, the stipends and equipment for the scholars will not be affected by this budget cut. Instead, the building of infrastructures will be delayed.
