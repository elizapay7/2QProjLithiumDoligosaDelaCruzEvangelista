What is PSHS-WVC

PSHS-WVC stands for Philippine Science High School - Western Visayas Campus. It is a prestigious public high school located in Iloilo City, Philippines, specializing in STEM education for gifted students from Western Visayas and beyond. Philippine Science High School is a research-oriented and specialized public high school system in the Philippines that operates as an attached agency of the Philippine DOST.
PSHS-WVC boasts a team of well-qualified and experienced teachers passionate about STEM education. PSHS-WVC offers a challenging academic curriculum designed to nurture the talent and potential of students in STEM fields. PSHS-WVC is a top-notch institution dedicated to equipping future leaders in STEM disciplines with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to excel and contribute to society.

Should my child/ward go to this school?

This school/institution offers the BEST high school education in the whole country. Your child will not only receive the best education, but will also be influenced by the teachers and the community.

When did it start operating?

PSHS-WVC was the 3rd campus to be established. It was founded on July 1, 1992. On June 13, 1997, the school eventually had its formal inaugural ceremony to officially launch its activities. Its doors were opened by 47 trailblazing academics who were excited to start their special scientific journey on the brand-new campus.

What are the perks of this institution?

All the students going to this institution are considered scholars or “iskolar ng bayan”. They also will receive quality education, NO expenses and a monthly stipend depending on the parents' income.
